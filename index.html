隐私政策

Privacy Policy

最后更新：2026年7月

Last Updated: July 2026

1. 核心承诺

Yearly12（一页知年）是一款 100% 离线运行的应用。无需联网、无需注册账号、无需登录。您的所有数据永远只保存在您自己的设备上，不会上传到任何服务器。仅在以下场景会使用网络：App Store 评价跳转和应用内购买验证。

2. 信息收集

我们不收集任何个人信息。应用内所有数据均为您主动输入的内容：

  • 您输入的目标、记录和文章内容
  • 您选择的设置（主题、年份偏好等）
  • 购买记录（通过 Apple 处理，我们不存储支付信息）

3. 信息存储与安全

  • 所有数据使用 SwiftData 本地数据库存储
  • 图片存储在应用沙盒内
  • 无云端同步，无数据传输
  • 断网状态下完全可用
  • 导出功能仅将文件保存到您自己的设备

4. 数据分享

本应用没有分享功能。导出的文件（PDF/HTML/JSON）仅保存到您本地设备的「文件」应用中，不会自动发送给任何第三方。您完全掌控自己的数据流向。

5. 第三方服务

  • Apple StoreKit：仅用于处理应用内购买
  • 无其他第三方服务或 SDK
  • 无广告、无追踪、无数据分析

6. 您的权利

  • 随时导出您的所有数据（JSON 格式）
  • 随时在「设置 → 删除所有数据」中永久删除全部内容
  • 随时在系统设置中关闭通知权限
  • 卸载应用即自动清除所有数据

7. 权限说明

  • 通知权限：用于月度记录提醒和周度目标回顾提醒，您可在系统设置中随时关闭
  • 照片库权限：仅在您主动点击「插图」按钮选择图片时访问，图片仅保存在本地设备

8. 儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。

9. 隐私政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何更改将在本页面公布。

10. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过 App Store 的「开发者网站」联系我们。

1. Core Commitment

Yearly12 is a 100% offline application. No internet required, no account needed, no login. All your data stays on your device and is never uploaded to any server. Network is only used for App Store review redirects and in-app purchase verification.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information. All data in the app is content you actively provide:

  • Goals, journals, and writing content you enter
  • Settings you choose (theme, year preference, etc.)
  • Purchase records (processed by Apple; we do not store payment info)

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally using SwiftData
  • Images are stored within the app sandbox
  • No cloud sync, no data transmission
  • Fully functional offline
  • Export saves files only to your device

4. Data Sharing

This app has no sharing feature. Exported files (PDF/HTML/JSON) are saved only to the Files app on your device and are never automatically sent to any third party. You have full control over your data.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit: Used only for in-app purchases
  • No other third-party services or SDKs
  • No ads, no tracking, no analytics

6. Your Rights

  • Export all your data at any time (JSON format)
  • Permanently delete everything via Settings → Delete All Data
  • Disable notification permissions in system settings at any time
  • Uninstalling the app automatically removes all data

7. Permissions

  • Notifications: Used for monthly journal reminders and weekly goal reviews. You can disable this in system settings at any time.
  • Photo Library: Accessed only when you tap the Images button. Images are stored locally on your device only.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the developer website on the App Store.
